Guacamole

Ingredients

  1. 3 avocados
  2. 1 lime, juiced
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. half cup onion diced
  5. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  6. 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  7. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  8. 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Preparation

  • Start by mashing your avacados
  • add Lime juice and salt and mix
  • then you can add all the other ingredients and make sure you mix it well then eat.

This is a great party snack to have when your watching a movie or a football game with friends. It is a quick and easy snack that you can bring to all ocasions.
